Reklaim Ltd.

Business summary

  • Privacy-first data platform enabling compliant, user-consented consumer data for brands, agencies, platforms, and data companies
  • Two-sided consumer offering: Reklaim Rewards (data monetization) and Reklaim Protect (privacy protection subscription)
  • B2B distribution across data/advertising ecosystem (including SSPs and direct integration with The Trade Desk)
  • Revenue from identity data and platform/audience segment sales; expanding into recurring consumer subscriptions

Recent developments

  • Q2 2025 revenue up 18% YoY to $1.30M; identity revenue more than doubled YoY; gross margin 77%
  • EBITDA improved YoY in Q2 to -$141K (from -$333K); Q2 net loss -$224K; H1 net loss -$430K
  • Debentures fully repaid by June 24, 2025; cash at quarter-end $419K; significant warrant exercises and expiries reduced overhang
  • Launched Reklaim Protect (May 2025) adding consumer subscription revenue; automated data-broker opt-outs and breach alerts
  • Direct integration with The Trade Desk to recapture margin previously shared with a third-party distributor
  • Regulatory tailwinds: eight more U.S. states enacted privacy laws in 2025 (total 20 states), increasing demand for compliant, consented data

Key issues

  • Going concern uncertainty disclosed (low cash balance, negative YTD operating cash flow, reliance on additional financing if needed)
  • Liquidity and small-cap market conditions; focus on profitability, share buybacks, and measured growth
  • Customer concentration and credit risk management; allowance for expected credit losses maintained
  • FX exposure to USD flows; a 10% CAD/USD move estimated to impact earnings by ~$476K
  • Execution on scaling Protect subscriptions and B2B shelf space while maintaining margins
