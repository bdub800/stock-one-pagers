BluMetric Environmental Inc.

Business summary

  • Provider of water technologies and environmental consulting across WaterTech (treatment systems) and Professional Services (engineering, monitoring, H&S, remediation, resources/geomatics).
  • Serves four core markets: Commercial & Industrial, Government (incl. Northern Canada), Military, and Mining.
  • Operates from 10 offices and 2 manufacturing facilities in Canada and the U.S., with ~230 employees.
  • Differentiates via integrated offerings: turnkey water/wastewater systems plus full-cycle environmental and health & safety services.

Recent developments

  • Closed acquisition of Gemini Water LLC (Sep 23, 2024), expanding fixed-base desalination/wastewater systems and U.S./Caribbean footprint; driving large revenue growth in WaterTech.
  • Expanded WaterTech fabrication capacity to >40,000 sq ft across Canada and the U.S.; ramped Mission Ready Water and military systems (e.g., ASWUPS for Rheinmetall).
  • Strengthened Toronto presence and U.S. leadership to grow Commercial & Industrial and introduce recurring O&M revenue models.
  • Increased operating line availability to $4.0M; no borrowings outstanding as of Jun 30, 2025; net cash improved.

Key issues

  • Professional Services softness due to delayed Canadian Federal Government contract awards and general market uncertainty; lower utilization pressured margins and operating costs.
  • Sales mix shift toward WaterTech reduced consolidated gross margin (higher hardware mix vs. services).
  • Mining market slowdown; Government revenue timing sensitive to political cycles and project starts.
  • Typical seasonality in Professional Services, while WaterTech varies with commercial demand timing.
