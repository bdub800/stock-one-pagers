BIOREM Inc. (BRM.V)

Business summary

  • Clean technology company designing and manufacturing biological air emission control systems for odor, VOC and HAP removal, plus biogas conditioning.
  • Core market: North American municipal wastewater; global footprint via reps, with manufacturing and sales in North America and China.
  • Over 2,000 installed systems; custom engineered solutions with dedicated service and support.
  • R&D focus on media technologies and biological treatment of hydrogen sulfide.

Recent developments

  • Q2 2025 revenue rose 29% YoY to $9.43M; gross margin expanded to ~27% (vs ~22.7% in Q2 2024).
  • H1 2025 bookings reached $24.1M (+27% YoY), with Q2 bookings of $7.4M; backlog at quarter-end was $60.9M (+$3.9M YoY).
  • Q2 2025 G&A included a $411k provision for potential uncollectability on a specific project with funding issues.
  • Cash decreased to $7.66M (from $9.60M at Mar 31, 2025) as working capital absorbed cash; Q2 operating cash flow was -$1.34M; capex of $0.25M.
  • Project execution improved despite logistics/geopolitical/tariff headwinds; management expects continued revenue conversion from backlog over the next ~18 months.

Key issues

  • Backlog conversion and customer scheduling drive quarterly volatility; long sales cycles can cause revenue and cash flow swings.
  • Customer payment delays/default risk can pressure liquidity; fixed-price contract risk if actual costs exceed estimates.
  • Dependence on subcontractors for field construction, and ongoing exposure to FX (US-dollar monetary assets ~US$5.8M at June 30, 2025).
  • Product development and market acceptance risks for new media technologies; low share-trading liquidity can amplify price moves.
