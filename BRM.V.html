BIOREM Inc.

Business summary

  • Designs and manufactures biological air emissions control systems (odor, VOC, HAPs) and biogas conditioning solutions
  • Custom-engineered systems with >2,000 installed globally; core market is North American municipal sector
  • Operations include sales/manufacturing in North America and China, a research facility, global rep network, and dedicated service division
  • Uses long-term, fixed-price contracts with percentage-of-completion revenue recognition

Recent developments

  • Q2 2025 revenue rose to $9.4M (+99% q/q; +29% y/y) with gross margin improving to 27%
  • Strong order activity YTD: H1 2025 bookings $24.1M (+27% vs H1 2024); Q2 bookings $7.4M
  • Backlog at June 30, 2025: $60.9M (down $7.2M q/q on execution; up $3.9M y/y); majority expected to convert within 18 months
  • Provision recorded: $411k in G&A for a specific project’s potential collection risk
  • Liquidity: cash of $7.7M; working capital $10.0M; $3.0M undrawn operating line; invested $249k in manufacturing equipment in Q2

Key issues

  • Project timing and fixed-price exposure: revenue and gross profit depend on customer schedules and cost control
  • Collection and funding risk: heightened by long project cycles; a specific project required a bad-debt provision in Q2
  • Supply chain and geopolitics: improving schedules, but ongoing uncertainty could affect delivery and margins
  • FX exposure: US-dollar net monetary assets ~$5.8M at June 30, 2025; earnings subject to FX swings
  • Market concentration: heavy reliance on US and Canadian municipal markets and subcontractor availability
